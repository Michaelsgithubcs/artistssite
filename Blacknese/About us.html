What is Blackanese TV?

Blackanese TV is a platform that focuses on shining a light on South African fashion and helping shape and elevate the SA fashion culture. Our focus is entirely on fashion-based content and other micro-industries that fall under the fashion umbrella, from make-up, styling, fashion photography, creative directing, etc.

Our Vision:

To create opportunities, creative spaces, growth, and impact in the South African creative industry. To be the world's leading creative centre.

Our Mission:

To inspire every creative in South Africa to achieve more through their craft. We aim to create more spaces that cater to creatives and upcoming South African fashion brands.
